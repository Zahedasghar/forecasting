Professor Dr. Zahid Asghar

Dr. Asghar is currently serving as Professor at School of Economics, Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad. He has also served at National Institute of Banking and Finance, State Bank of Pakistam, for two years as a senior faculty. He has also served Quaid-i-Azam University as Registrar from 2020 to 2021 , Director School of Economics from 2019-2022, Chairman Department of Statistics from 2017-2018 at Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad.

Professor Asghar is an applied econometrician and statistician with special interests in development economics, making sense of data through Data visualization and have conducted a large number of trainings on data analytics using Excel, STATA, R and EVIEWS. He has expertise in using R , STATA and Excel , and comfortable in using Datawrapper, Tableau, SPSS and EVIEWS for data anlysis. He has conducted more than 40 training workshops for professionals, researchers, policy makers, mid career professionals at various institutes NIMs, SBP, PIDE, International Rescue Committee, TEVTA,Universities and others. He is actively engaged in playing with data particularly PovCalnetR, PDHS, PSLM, WDI using R among many other survey data sets. He has published in gender, food security and other socio-economic issues both in national and international peer reviewed journals. He is keen in having better awareness of data analytics in the society and to contribute to an evidence based decision making in socio-economic policy. Professor Asghar is also a member of Research Advisory Committee for Research for Social Transformation and Action (RASTA), PIDE, Islamabad.
